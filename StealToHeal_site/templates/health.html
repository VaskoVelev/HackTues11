HEALTH

HEALTH ORGANIZATION

A well-structured health plan improves overall well-being. Maintain a balanced diet, exercise regularly, and get enough sleep. Stay hydrated, manage stress, and schedule routine health check-ups. Limiting screen time and maintaining work-life balance also contribute to a healthier lifestyle.




*Follow a Healthy Diet: Plan meals in advance and focus on balanced nutrition.

*Exercise Regularly: Schedule at least 30 minutes of physical activity 4-5 times a week.

*Get Enough Sleep: Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night.

*Stay Hydrated: Drink at least 2 liters of water daily.

*Manage Stress: Practice mindfulness, meditation, or deep breathing exercises.

*Regular Health Check-Ups: Schedule medical check-ups to monitor overall health.

*Limit Screen Time: Reduce blue light exposure, especially before bedtime.

*Maintain Work-Life Balance: Set boundaries between work and personal life to avoid burnout.
